A DANCER WHO DANCES WITH A HEART ONLY.

I am Valeriia Prokopenko, a dancer, performer and artist living in Klaipeda, Lithuania.

My journey as a professional artist has been shaped by my experiences with Dside Dance Studio and "Astra" studio in Ukraine, where I trained in various dance styles and techniques. As both a solo and partner dancer, I thrive on performing and connecting with audiences, having worked with renowned choreographers like Oleksandra Holushko and Volodymir Goncharov. My studies in contemporary and classical dance have deepened my understanding of different art forms, and as I approach the end of my training, I seek new opportunities to collaborate with artists and expand my creative and technical horizons.
